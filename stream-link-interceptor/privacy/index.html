Privacy Policy for Stream Link Interceptor

Effective Date: 9/3/2024

Data Collection:

Stream Link Interceptor does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not track your activity, browsing history, or any other identifiable information.

OAuth Authorization:

The extension uses OAuth to connect directly to your music streaming accounts. Authorization tokens are stored locally on your device and are used solely for the purpose of translating links between services. We do not have access to or store these tokens on any server.

Communication with Streaming Services:

All communications between the extension and music streaming services occur directly between your browser and the serviceâ€™s API. No data is sent to or processed by any third-party servers.

Data Security:

Your authorization tokens are stored securely on your device. We recommend using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication on your accounts for additional security.
